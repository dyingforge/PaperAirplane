ğŸ§© How It Works

Create a Paper Plane

Users can create a paper plane with a short message or comment.

Once created, the paper plane is sent out into the system, ready to be picked up by others.

Pick a Random Paper Plane

Users can browse and pick a random paper plane.

After picking, they can leave a comment on it, adding their own thoughts.

Then, they send it off into the system again, waiting for someone else to pick it up.

Receive Paper Planes

After a set period of time, users will receive their originally sent paper planes back.

The paper plane now contains comments and messages left by others, giving a delightful surprise and a glimpse of how their ideas were perceived.

Walrus Integration

The system uses Walrus for decentralized storage of the paper planes and comments, ensuring that all messages and interactions are secure and immutable.

Seal helps in managing the randomness of paper plane assignment and comment distribution.
